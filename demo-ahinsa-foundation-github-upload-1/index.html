Before you begin

New to Reiki? Here are the things people most often ask Mrs. Anju.

What happens in a Reiki session?

You simply rest, fully clothed and comfortable, while Mrs. Anju gently channels healing energy through her hands. Most people feel a deep calm, warmth and lightness. Many drift into peaceful relaxation.

Does distance healing really work?

Yes. Energy is not bound by distance. A distance healing is just as powerful as being in the same room, and you receive it from the comfort of your own home, anywhere in the world.

Do I need to believe in Reiki for it to help?

Not at all. Reiki works whether or not you believe in it. Come with an open heart and let the energy do the rest.

Is Reiki safe alongside my medicines or treatment?

Reiki is completely safe, gentle and natural. It sits beautifully alongside any medical treatment. It supports your wellbeing and never replaces your doctor's advice.

How many sessions will I need?

Everyone is different. Some feel lighter after a single session; others love coming regularly. Mrs. Anju will gently guide you to what feels right for you.

Can anyone learn Reiki?

Absolutely, anyone, at any age, can learn. Mrs. Anju teaches every level, from Reiki Level 1 all the way to Grand Master.
